Terms & Conditions

Terms and conditions for enrolling in the Internship Training Programme are as follows:

Candidates joining the program must pay the full amount or opt for a quarterly payment plan. However, the full payment must be completed within 15 to 25 days. Please note that the amount paid for the course is non-refundable.

Grow Wake will provide placement support to candidates who complete their courses. Candidates availing of placement services will be required to pay 8% + GST of their first year's CTC (Cost to Company) as a placement fee.

  • 50% of the Placement Fee must be paid within the first 5 days of joining the company.
  • The remaining 50% must be paid within 30 days of joining the company.

All payments are subject to Goods and Services Tax (GST) at the applicable rate. Grow Wake will issue an invoice for the placement fee, which must be paid within the timelines mentioned above or any extended period approved by the company.

The candidate shall remain liable to pay the entire placement fee, even if they leave the employment after accepting the offer. In case of non-payment within the stipulated time, interest will be charged on the outstanding amount at a rate of 12% per annum for each day's delay.

We offer an internship program with opportunities to work on current projects, providing valuable hands-on experience. Upon successful completion, candidates will receive an internship certificate. Please note that this is a paid internship and Grow Wake does not provide laptops or systems for the program.

To earn an internship or course completion certificate, candidates must participate in and complete mock interviews.
